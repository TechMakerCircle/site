Tech Maker Circle

YEG

TMC is...

A monthly gathering for education and networking in the Edmonton tech community. Whether you're a hobbyist, student, or a professional, Tech Maker Circle is a monthly excuse to meet up and learn new things from your local community.

We're not focused on specific technologies or disciplines; we simply believe there's lots to be learned from mixing the broader community together.

The event is free. Food and drink are provided.
